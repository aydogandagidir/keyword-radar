# Known Limitations

1. Marketplace autocomplete behavior can change without notice.
2. DOM selectors may require maintenance.
3. Search volume is not guaranteed or exact in MVP.
4. Suggestions are based on what the marketplace UI exposes to the user.
5. Some marketplaces may throttle rapid autocomplete interactions.
6. Extension behavior may vary depending on A/B tests, login state, region, or cookies.
7. Backend skeleton is MVP-ready but not a full SaaS billing/auth system.
8. AI clustering is prepared architecturally but not enabled by default.
